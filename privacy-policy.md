# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

* Email address
* Authentication identifiers
* Account profile information you choose to provide

Authentication and account management are handled by a third-party authentication provider.

### Usage Information

We may collect information about how the App is used, including:

* App interactions and feature usage
* Device type and operating system
* App version
* Diagnostic and performance data

### Payment and Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription or paid feature, payment processing is handled by a third-party billing platform. We do not store your credit card details.

The billing provider may process:

* Purchase history
* Subscription status
* Transaction identifiers
* Country or region information

### Analytics Information

We may collect anonymized or aggregated analytics data to understand how users interact with the App and to improve performance and features.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Provide and operate the App
* Authenticate users and manage accounts
* Process subscriptions and purchases
* Improve functionality and user experience
* Monitor performance and troubleshoot issues
* Communicate important service updates

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on trusted third-party services that may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. These services may include:

* Authentication and user management services
* Subscription and in-app purchase management services
* Analytics and usage monitoring services
* Cloud infrastructure providers

These providers process data only as necessary to deliver their services.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no internet-based service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill legal or operational requirements. Information associated with accounts may be removed when an account is deleted or upon user request, subject to applicable obligations.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Request access to your data
* Request correction of inaccurate data
* Request deletion of your account or data
* Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be submitted using the contact information below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that personal data from a child has been collected, we will take steps to remove that information.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer and processing of information in jurisdictions that may have different data protection laws.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.
